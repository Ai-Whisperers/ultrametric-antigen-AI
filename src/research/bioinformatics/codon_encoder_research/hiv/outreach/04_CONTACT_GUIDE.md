# How to Contact: The Outreach Guide

## 1. Medicines Patent Pool (MPP)

- **Target Role:** Business Development or Scientific Manager.
- **Email:** `bd@medicinespatentpool.org`
- **Alternative:** Search LinkedIn for "Scientific Manager MPP Geneva".
- **Website:** [medicinespatentpool.org](https://medicinespatentpool.org/)

## 2. Drugs for Neglected Diseases initiative (DNDi)

- **Target Role:** R&D Director or Discovery team.
- **Email:** `enquiries@dndi.org` (Ask to be routed to R&D).
- **Website:** [dndi.org](https://dndi.org/)

## 3. Stanford HIV Drug Resistance Database

- **Target:** Prof. Robert Shafer.
- **Lab URL:** [hivdb.stanford.edu](https://hivdb.stanford.edu/)
- **Method:** Academic email (usually found on recent papers). Look for the corresponding author on the latest "HIVDB Update" paper.

## 4. Dr. Carlos Brizuela (CICESE)

- **Institution:** CICESE (Mexico).
- **Focus:** Computer Science / Bioinformatics.
- **Method:** Use the email from his "StarPep" publication or university profile.
