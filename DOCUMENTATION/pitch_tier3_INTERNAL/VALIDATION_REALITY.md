## HONEST EXTERNAL COMMUNICATION

### What We CAN Say

**Strong claims** (Level 1-2):
- "Our encoding captures evolutionary relationships without biological training"
- "Predictions correlate with structural predictions from AlphaFold3"
- "Distance metrics align with clinical resistance patterns"
- "The approach identifies sites independently validated by structure prediction"

**Measured claims** (Level 2-3):
- "We've identified candidate sites for further validation"
- "Computational analysis suggests [X], pending experimental confirmation"
- "The geometric framework generates testable hypotheses"

### What We CANNOT Say

**Overclaims to avoid**:
- "Validated" without specifying validation type
- "Proven" for anything beyond Level 1
- "Confirmed" for Level 3-4 items
- Implying wet-lab validation exists when it doesn't

## VALIDATION EVIDENCE SUMMARY

### The Core Demonstration

The system is not a toy because:

1. **Unsupervised structure emerges**: Without any biological labels, the encoding produces embeddings where known biological relationships appear (chemical classes separate, synonymous codons cluster)

2. **Blind predictions match physics**: Glycan sites we flag computationally cause structural perturbation when independently modeled by AlphaFold3

3. **Rankings match clinical reality**: Our constraint hierarchy for drug classes matches decades of clinical observation about resistance emergence

4. **Conservation patterns align**: Epitopes we identify as "high barrier" are indeed conserved in global sequence databases

### What This Proves

The geometric encoding captures **something real** about evolutionary constraints. The question is whether this translates to therapeutic utility - that requires wet-lab and clinical validation.

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

### In Pitches
- Don't say "validated" without qualifier
- Don't imply wet-lab data exists
- Don't conflate computational with experimental

### In Publications
- Clearly state "in silico" or "computational"
- Include "awaiting experimental confirmation"
- Don't overstate AlphaFold3 as "experimental" (it's also computational)

### In Partnerships
- Be upfront about validation status
- Position honestly as early-stage platform
- Let the correlations speak for themselves

## THE HONEST PITCH

**What we have**: A mathematical framework that encodes biological sequences in a geometry where distance predicts evolutionary cost. This encoding, without any biological training, produces predictions that correlate with:
- AlphaFold3 structural predictions
- Global sequence conservation patterns
- Clinical drug resistance hierarchies

**What this suggests**: The geometry captures real biological constraints.

**What we need**: Experimental partners to test whether these computational insights translate to therapeutic value.

**What we're NOT claiming**: That we've cured anything, validated anything in wet lab, or proven clinical utility.
