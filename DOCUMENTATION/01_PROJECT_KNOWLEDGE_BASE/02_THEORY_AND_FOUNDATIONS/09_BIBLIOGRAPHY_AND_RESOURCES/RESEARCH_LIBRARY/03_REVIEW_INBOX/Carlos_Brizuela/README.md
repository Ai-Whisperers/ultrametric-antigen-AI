# Carlos Brizuela

**Affiliation:** CICESE (Centro de Investigación Científica y de Educación Superior de Ensenada), Department of Computer Sciences.

**Research Areas:**

- Bioinformatics & Antimicrobial Peptide design
- Machine Learning for drug discovery
- Evolutionary algorithms & multi‑objective optimization
- Antenna and scheduling optimization (earlier work)

**Key Contributions:**

- Development of the **StarPep Toolbox**, an open‑source platform for chemical‑space analysis of bioactive peptides.
- Pioneering computational frameworks for tailored peptide and protein design (key‑cutting machine approach).
- Application of evolutionary strategies to engineering problems (phased arrays, job‑shop scheduling).
