# Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA) & HLA Geometry

**Status**: Clinical Validation
**Codebase**: `research/bioinformatics/codon_encoder_research/rheumatoid_arthritis/`

## Overview

Correlating HLA-DRB1 allele geometry with RA severity. This study aims to provide a prognostic tool for clinicians.

## Key Metrics

- **Hyperbolic Distance**: Distance from the "Healthy Self" centroid.
- **Epitope Spreading**: Predicting the trajectory of autoimmune targets over time.
